About us

Video Streaming

Live stream and on demand videos hosted by clinicians, to provide live health services and information.

All the clinicians on the platform are verified and registered with the GMC. Providing up to date and evidence based answers to your questions.

Health Communities

Connect with other people experiencing a similar health journey to you.

believe that every health experience is very unique and difficult, but what about finding others who understand your personal experience and are able to support you?

Our Vision

We understand the dangers of health misinformation online. By facilitating a forum to ask questions to clinicians and other patients, we want you to be confident about your health journey and make informed decisions with your clinicians.

Using technology, we aim to provide solutions to some of the challenges of accessing the national health services.
